Join Our Sustainability Challenges!

Participate in these challenges to promote environmental sustainability on campus. Every small effort counts!

1. Plastic Free Week

Challenge yourself to go an entire week without using any plastic products. Document your journey and share tips with others!

2. Water Saving Challenge

Track your water usage for a week and find ways to reduce it. Share your results and tips with your peers.

3. Eco-Friendly Commute

Try walking, biking, or carpooling to campus for a month. Encourage your friends to join and track the carbon emissions saved!

4. Green Space Restoration

Join a community event to plant trees or restore green spaces in your area. Share your experiences with your community!
