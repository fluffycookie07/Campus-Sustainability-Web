Why Water Conservation Matters

Water conservation is critical to ensuring a sustainable campus. By saving water, we can reduce our environmental footprint and conserve valuable resources.

Simple Ways to Save Water

  • Fix leaks in taps and toilets.
  • Take shorter showers.
  • Use a broom instead of a hose to clean driveways.
